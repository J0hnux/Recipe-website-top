Thor's Lasagna

Description

An Asgardian type of lasagna which can only be found in Asgard or the realms of Norse Mythology. Thor's lasagna, as the name suggests comes from the name of the God of Thunder, Thor.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp of Thor's magical sweat or any Asgardian oil
  • 1 withering garlic clove
  • 1 kilo Asgardian wheat flour
  • 4 pieces of Earth's most finest Tomato puree
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Put the 2 tbsp of Thor's sweat or any Asgardian oil on a hot pan
  2. Once the sweat or oil is hot, add the withering garlic clove and wait until it's golden brown.
  3. Mix the Asgardian wheat flour thoroughly until it magically turns into lasagna pieces.
  4. Put the Earth's finest tomato puree on the pan and wait until it's cooked.
  5. Add the salt and let it cook for 10 more minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
